PyCon UK 2018

15–19 September in Cardiff City Hall

PyCon UK is the annual gathering of the UK Python community and its friends from around the world. For a third year, PyCon UK is returning to Cardiff City Hall, from Saturday 15th to Wednesday 19th September.

If you've got any questions, please get in touch.

We look forward to seeing you in September!

~ The PyCon UK Committee
